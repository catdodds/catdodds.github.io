Community-Based Outpatient Clinics (CBOCs) and Primary Care Telehealth Outpatient Clinics (PTOCs)

Connect with your team to support your physical health.

Maintaing your health no matter your location: You should be able to reside wherever you like and still have access to VA services no matter where your location. Our Community-Based Outpatient Clinics and Primary Care Telehealth Outpatient Clinics allow you to get the help you need. Find the clinic nearest you below.

CBOCs and PTOCs

No matter if you go to a CBOC, PTOC, or the The Bob Stump VA Medical Center, each patient is part of the Northern Arizona VA Health Care System family. We work together to provide excellent health care so serve we can those who have fought and served for us. Click the image to see some of the other available VA facilities within NAVAHCS.
