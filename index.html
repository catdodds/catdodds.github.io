WHY VET TECHS?

Its easy to connect veterans to the resources they need without the hassle...making it so every vet can be a technology expert!

Our Goal: Putting the power of technology and online resources into your hands, easily, and more accessibly than searching through the Northern Arizona VA Health Care System website. While it is a great resource, that will have additional information, it can still be hard to find what you are looking for. This site has become a compilation of links and information for key areas such as Primary Care, Mental Health, Pharmacy, Homeless Resources, and other applications and websites that can be of use to veterans. Explore the menu above to see available resources. COVID-19 has changed the way we live, but should not change the ease of access to our healthcare.

About Me

I am a NAU student who is currently working on my senior captsone project as well as working for the Northern Arizona VA Health Care System at the Prescott location. I love technology and teaching those around me how to use it. Thus, the inspiration to make a site that has easy to find key links. I hope that Veterans who are able to utilize this site see it as a helpful tool.
