Mental Health

Connect with your provider to support your mental health.

Maintaining your mind: Mental Health is important. Whether seeing a provider for therapy, medication management, or even in a crisis, we are here for you. Mental Health Services provide intake assessments, medication management and individual and group psychotherapy.

Are you a Veteran in crisis?

If you are a veteran in crisis. Please click the image above to connect with the Veteran's Crisis line website. Or visit us for a crisis walk in at our Prescott campus, available Monday through Friday, 8:00 to 16:00 to speak with someone on our team. Crisis walk in clinic is located in building 161, second floor.
